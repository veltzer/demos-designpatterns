# Identify patterns in the following exapmles from the JDK...

In each case state if the pattern deviates in any way from the "stardard" implementation of the pattern.
Remember that a link may be a part of more than one pattern. Try to identify them all.
Sometimes the names of classess reveal one of their design patterns blueprints.
